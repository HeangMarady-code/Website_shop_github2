FAQ

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days depending on your location.
What materials are your shirts made from?
All THREAD tees are made from 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton. We use water-based inks that are soft to the touch and last wash after wash.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track it directly on our website or via the carrier's site.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and times vary by destination. Import duties may apply depending on your country.
How do I care for my THREAD tee?
Machine wash cold, inside out, on a gentle cycle. Tumble dry low or hang to dry. Do not bleach. Iron on low heat if needed. Avoid direct ironing on the print.
